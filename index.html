Apple, a very renowned name is the technology market was founded by the great personality Steve Jobs along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne in 1976 and its headquarters is in Cupertino, California. They began their journey with making personal computers but now this consumer electronics giant makes everything starting from laptops to portable media players. The very first world-changing iPhone came into the market in 2007 and took over the world. Since then, Apple has released 11 generations of iPhone, accompanied with 11 releases of IOS operating system. iPhone is considered as a game changer for the industry of smartphones. The success of iPhone helped Apple to become the world’s most important and valuable company. Apple smartphones are considered as first-class luxury items because of its skyrocketing prices. The company is known best for manufacturing attractive and intuitive experiences for the users with products that are elegant, yet simple. Apple values its customers the most and takes pride in being known for producing the most durable products, and retaining their customers for longer times.
